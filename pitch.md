# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot - Pitch

## Problem Statement
The old menu-based student portal is inefficient because it requires students to navigate through multiple pages just to find simple information. This process is time-consuming, confusing, and not user-friendly, especially for new students. It also lacks personalization and does not adapt to student needs.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot empowers students by assisting them in accessing information quickly through intelligent responses. Instead of replacing human interaction, it enhances student experience by providing guidance, reducing stress, and supporting decision-making. It focuses on human-centered technology where AI acts as a helpful assistant.

## Agile User Stories

1. As a student, I want to ask the AI about my clearance status so that I can quickly know if I have pending requirements.

2. As a student, I want to check my class schedule using AI so that I don’t need to manually search through menus.

3. As a student, I want to receive announcements from the AI so that I stay updated with campus activities.
